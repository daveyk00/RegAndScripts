Set 'Account lockout duration' to 15 minutes
Set 'Account lockout threshold' to 10
Set 'Account lockout reset' to 15 minutes

Create configuration policy of custom:

Name: DeviceLockAccountLockoutPolicy
Description: Configure lockout duration to 15, threshold to 10 and reset after 15 minutes
OMA-URI: ./Device/Vendor/MSFT/Policy/Config/DeviceLock/AccountLockoutPolicy
Data type: String
Value:
AccountLockoutDuration:15, AccountLockoutThreshold:10, ResetAccountLockoutCounterAfter:15
